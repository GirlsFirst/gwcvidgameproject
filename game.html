OUR SCRATCH GAME

This is a scratch game that the team created! Feel free to try it out!

GAME INSTRUCTIONS:

You are the detective trying to handcuff (and arrest) the (mafia) murderer. Listen to the story carefully for clues and use them to your advantage to find the possible (mafia) murderer! GAME CONTROLS: Use the left arrow to move left and the right arrow to move left.
