Our Project

"Our inspiration for our project came from our time inside the classroom when we would bond through games and non-coding activities. The game “Mafia” was the greatest example of this; during our third week of the program, we were usually done early with our code for robotics, so we decided to play “Mafia”. It definitely impacted our idea of our game and placed us closer to sisterhood! By the way, we chose purple as our color scheme due to its mysteriousness."
